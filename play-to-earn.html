Orbit Metaverse's Play To Earn business model is developed based on the basic rules of a revolving economy where the team will not talk about players having to do tasks and receive bonuses anymore, but behind them are aspects of the player's career when they make decisions for themselves. They could be an online blacksmith, a high-ranking person, a gem collector, an auction trader, or someone who focuses on finding little fortunes...

The Orbit Metaverse team focuses on developing micro-economic community groups, and solving different supply and demand problems for different types of player, etc. This is the way we establish and form such a miniature society.
