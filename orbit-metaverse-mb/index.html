GAME PLAY

SINGLE PLAYER

The basic playing mode of the game. A player go on a lone journey and conquer challenges, explore more lands and get rewards in return.

PVP

The arena between players. The players compete for rewards to across each land. You can enter the arena and choose your opponent to fight.

PVE

The campaign mode. The players must use their knowledge to use the right characters and equipment to fight the boss. The players can participate in battles of varying difficulty.
